Ghana And Video Games Project

Research initiative exploring the gaming culture in Ghana

Research Initiative

The "Ghana And Video Games" project is a research initiative that I have started to explore the Ghanaian perspective on Video Games and Video Game Development in general. Through comprehensive surveys and community engagement, I hope to be able to document perceptions of video games and game development across Ghana.

Research Objectives

  • Map the landscape of gaming perception across different demographics in Ghana
  • Identify barriers to entry for aspiring Ghanaian game developers
  • Explore the potential of games as tools for cultural expression and education further down the line, if research bears fruit
  • Assess market and sponsor opportunities for locally developed games reflecting Ghanaian narratives

Current Progress

I currently have started my first Survey to get the very brief and generalised idea and habits that we have around Gaming. It is going to be a long way from here, but I will keep at it. The survey touches on: Technological access, perception of game development as a career, and interest in games that represent local culture. Initial findings are yet to be finalized as I just started the Survey. So I really will appreciate you sharing or contributing to the G.A.V.G. project in anyway possible.

Future Impact

Based on the research, I (or we if it comes to that) may make a purely Ghana-made Video Game. Other actual impactful actions may be taken, depending on whether I have overcome my shyness or built up the Confidence to be able to do that.

Participate in the Survey if You haven't. I will appreciate it, a lot.
